Cryptocam allows you to take encrypted photos and videos so your data is safe even if an attacker gains
physical access to your device or SD card.

<h3>Find a full tutorial on <a href="https://cryptocam.gitlab.io">https://cryptocam.gitlab.io</a></h3>

Worried that your device or memory card might get into the wrong hands with sensitive footage on it?
Cryptocam uses <a href="https://github.com/FiloSottile/age">age</a> (X25519 and ChaCha20) to encrypt
recorded videos and photos without ever persisting unencrypted data to device storage.
